# Atlas AI Philosophy
Version: 1.0
Status: Draft

---

# Purpose

Artificial Intelligence is a component of Atlas.

It is not Atlas.

Atlas is the business platform.

Artificial Intelligence exists only to transform information between business layers.

---

# Core Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence never owns business logic.

Business logic always belongs to Atlas.

The AI only performs transformations.

---

# Responsibilities

Artificial Intelligence MAY

- Read emails
- Read PDFs
- Read spreadsheets
- Extract information
- Normalize information
- Classify information
- Generate customer communication

Artificial Intelligence MUST NOT

- Invent information
- Decide pricing
- Create business rules
- Decide operational workflows
- Replace business validation
- Replace PostgreSQL

---

# AI Layers

Atlas separates Artificial Intelligence into independent engines.

Parser

Converts raw communication into ATL.

Validator

Verifies that ATL satisfies business requirements.

Reply Generator

Transforms ATL into customer communication.

Knowledge Engine

Provides business context.

Each engine has one responsibility.

---

# Decision Making

Artificial Intelligence never makes business decisions.

Atlas makes business decisions.

Examples

AI extracts

Airport = QRO

Atlas decides

Airport Transfer

AI extracts

80 passengers

Atlas decides

Vehicle allocation

AI extracts

Wedding

Atlas decides

Operational workflow

---

# Long-Term Goal

Every AI model should be replaceable.

Replacing GPT with another model must not require changing Atlas.

The only requirement is producing valid ATL.

---

# Summary

AI translates.

Atlas decides.
